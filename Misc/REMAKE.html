Who We Are

A cooperative built on community and honest food

Founded in 2012, Greenfield Local Hub began as a small collective of five farms looking for a better way to reach local customers. Today we represent over 40 producers across the region, operating a retail outlet, an online shop and a collective marketing platform.

We believe passionately that knowing where your food comes from — and who grew it — makes it taste better. Our mission is to shorten the distance between farm and fork.

Our Principles

What we stand for

Transparency

Every product carries full traceability information. You can always see which farm it came from, how it was grown, and how far it has travelled.

Community

We are member-owned and community-run. Profits are reinvested in our producers and in the local food economy, not extracted by outside shareholders.

Sustainability

We prioritise producers who use regenerative or low-impact methods, reduce food miles, and minimise packaging waste across the supply chain.
